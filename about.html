A little about me

Greetings! My name is Alexa Díaz, and an “Ugly Betty” rerun paired with an appetite for local news dazzled me into wanting to be a proud brown girl in journalism. I reported for my small-town newsroom in Southern California and led the high school paper before venturing to Syracuse University. While studying journalism in central New York, I became the first woman of color to head The Daily Orange, the independent student-run news organization of Syracuse. After a few golden summers spent fact-checking and reporting in magazine offices, I’ll start my post-grad life as an intern at the Los Angeles Times.

I’m always happy to chat, answer any questions and help with anything I can. Thanks so much for taking the time. I hope you’ll consider supporting a news organization that serves you today. Support
