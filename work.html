“How 9/11 changed academic programming at Syracuse University”

The Daily Orange • Sept. 13, 2016

When researching how the Syracuse University community reacted to the 9/11 attacks, I saw a trend in the creation of niche academic programming. Here, I explore that phenomenon and retrace the history of the reactions on an institutional basis at SU fifteen years after the attacks. This story ran on the front page.
